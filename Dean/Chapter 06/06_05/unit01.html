Setting up your SubDomain

  1. Set up your own SubDomain on StateU.org and install a dedicated instance of WordPress to be used just for this course.
  2. Create your first post and introduce yourself to the course. Be sure to include why you are interested in taking this course and what types of Environmental Impacts interest you the most. Tag your post with: Introduction
  3. Submit the URL for your Blog via ENV432 Syndication Form.
  4. Personalize your site to make it your own. Keep in mind that this will be your public face for this course so be sure to represent yourself in a professional manner. If you need assistance in setting up or configuring your WordPress site be sure to check out one of the WordPress training courses on Lynda.com.

Class Discussion Prep

Review the lecture materials for Unit 01. Select a concept from the materials that you find particularly interesting, post picture to your blog related to the concept and write a brief blog post about the it. Be sure to include additional information in your post that was not contained in course materials. Be prepared to discuss your findings in class on Thursday.
