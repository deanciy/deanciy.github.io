Video Lecture

Guest Lecture: Dr Walter Hatch

Positive and Negative Impacts

Human activities are generally considered to have a negative impact on life. In this chapter we will look at both positive and negative impacts that humans have on life. Begin researching stories where human interventions changed a natural system and impacted life.
